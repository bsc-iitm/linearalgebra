## Question

If $Ax = b$ has a solution and $A^Ty = 0$, what is the value of $y^T b$?

## Solution




- Let $u$ be a solution to the system, then $Au=b$. 



- $b^{T} y=( Au)^{T} y=u^{T} A^{T} y=0$
